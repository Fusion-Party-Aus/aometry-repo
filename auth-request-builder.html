📋 Channel policy & guide ▼

Purpose

This channel is for authorising content only. Messages other than an auth request or a formal objection to proposed content will be deleted. Discussion belongs in threads.

Roles with access

@National Executive, @Policy Dev Committee, @Comms Committee, @authnational, @Candidate, @Comms

Submitting a request

  • Your message must ping @authnational.
  • Specify the platform and account you intend to post to.
  • Proofread before posting — check for typos.
  • Use Shift+Enter to add paragraph breaks for readability.
  • Posts from @Candidate or @Comms automatically receive voting buttons from the bot.

Approval process

  • @authnational is the only role that can approve (✅). Anyone in the channel may lodge a rejection (❌).
  • Once two approvals are registered, the bot posts a summary naming the approvers and pinging the requester.
  • If your content only requires one approver (besides yourself), approve your own request first — then only one additional approval is needed.
  • You are responsible for knowing how many approvals your content requires.

Rejections

  • If you reject a request, immediately start a thread or reply with the substance of your objection — or at least indicate when you will provide it.
  • Do not post content that has an unresolved rejection against it. Resolve the issue in a thread first.

Post format (social media)

The content block in your auth request should follow this structure:

<Your commentary> See our policy here: <policy link> <article link> #auspol #fusionparty #PolicyAreaTag

For Facebook and Instagram: attach a screenshot or image from the article. Do not rely on the link preview card.

Hashtag rules

  • All posts should include #auspol, #fusionparty, and a tag for the relevant policy area (e.g. #ClimateRescue, #SecularHumanism, #FutureFocused).
  • Additional hashtags should be topical and platform-appropriate. For example, #EndFossilFuels is fine on Twitter/X but not appropriate for Facebook.

Scheduling

  • Once approved, reply to the auth message to confirm scheduling.
  • Include the scheduled time and flag anything that still needs attention (e.g. a missing policy link, hashtag review) by tagging the relevant platform role.

Slowmode

This channel has a 2-hour slowmode. This limits post volume and keeps discussion out of the main channel. If you need more than one reply, move it to a thread — no exceptions.
